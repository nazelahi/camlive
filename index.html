Powerful Features for Professional Streaming

Everything you need to broadcast your snooker matches professionally

Multi-Platform Streaming

Stream simultaneously to YouTube, Facebook, Twitch, and custom RTMP/RTSP servers. Reach your audience wherever they are.

Real-Time Score Overlay

Professional score overlays that update in real-time. Designed specifically for snooker with frame tracking, break scores, and player statistics.

HD Quality Streaming

Stream in high definition with hardware-accelerated encoding. Support for H.264, H.265, and AV1 codecs.

Camera Controls

Full camera control with autofocus, exposure adjustment, and the ability to switch between front and back cameras during streaming.

Remote Control

Control your stream remotely via web interface. Update scores, manage stream settings, and monitor your broadcast from any device.

Scene Management

Create and switch between multiple scenes. Add custom overlays, images, and text to enhance your broadcast.

Recording & VOD

Record your streams locally or save them as YouTube VODs. Never lose a memorable match.

Audio Processing

Professional audio controls with microphone volume adjustment, noise gate, and dynamic compression for crystal-clear commentary.
